### **1st Cycle Degree - Bachelor**

#### :one: **First Year** 

- :star: [**Calculus**](1st%20Year/1st%20Semester/Cálculo) Introduction of the basic concepts and results of single-variable Calculus.
  <br>
  **Set of all Slides and Practical Exercises.**

- [**Functional Programming**](1st%20Year/1st%20Semester/Programação%20Funcional) Learning how to program in an Functional Setting using Haskell Language.
  <br>
  **Set of all Slides and Practical Exercises.**

- [**Informatics Laboratories I**](1st%20Year/1st%20Semester/Laboratórios%20de%20Informática%20I) Knowledge application about Haskell Language.

  **Puzzle Video Game like Sokoban developed in Haskell Language.**

- [**Linear Algebra EI**](1st%20Year/1st%20Semester/Álgebra%20Linear%20EI) Development basic knowledge on Linear Algebra. 
  <br>
  **Set of all Slides and Practical Exercises.**
  
- [**Topics of Systems Engineering**](1st%20Year/1st%20Semester/Elementos%20de%20Engenharia%20de%20Sistemas) Introduction of the area of Systems Engineering through its characterization, methodology, and representative tools.
  <br>
  **Set of all Slides and Practical Exercises.**
  <br>
  **Airpot Simulation Project developed on Software Arena.**

  <br>
  **Set of all solved Practical Exercises.**

  **Set of all Slides and Practical Exercises.**

  <br>
  **Set of all Slides.**


#### :two: **Second Year** 

- :boom: [**Algorithms and Complexity**](2nd%20Year/1st%20Semester/Algoritmos%20e%20Complexidade) The main focus is the analysis of algorithms, from two perspectives: correction and efficiency.
  <br>
  **Set of all solved Practical Exercises.**

- [**Applied Statistics**](2nd%20Year/1st%20Semester/Estatística%20Aplicada) Learn different techniques used in Statistic for data collection, initial treatment, and through adequate probabilistic outcomes to infer from a sample to the population.
  <br>
  **Set of all Slides and some Practical Exercises.**

- [**Computer Architecture**](2nd%20Year/1st%20Semester/Arquitetura%20de%20Computadores) Introduction of modern computer architecture, in particular, techniques for performance acceleration, including memory hierarchy, pipelining, super-scalar, code optimization, vector processing and multicore.
  <br>
  **Set of all Slides and Practical Exercises**

- [**Data Communication**](2nd%20Year/1st%20Semester/Comunicação%20de%20Dados) Knowledge about fundamental concepts associated with areas of data communications and communications/telecommunications systems.
  <br>
  **Set of all Slides and Practical Exercises.**
  
- [**Economics Engineering**](2nd%20Year/1st%20Semester/Engenharia%20Económica) Knowledge of economics and management in order to enable them a correct decision-making in a business environment.
  <br>
  **Set of all Slides and Practical Exercises.**
  
- [**Introduction to Dynamic Systems**](2nd%20Year/1st%20Semester/Introdução%20aos%20Sistemas%20Dinâmicos) Knowledge about Dynamical Systems, Ordinary Differential Equations and Partial Differential Equations.
  <br>
  **Class Notebook.**
  
  ---
  
- [**Electromagnetism EE**](2nd%20Year/2nd%20Semester/Eletromagnetismo) Learning elementary electromagnetism.
  <br>
  **Book with all Teorical and Practical and Theoretical information.**

- [**Informatics Laboratories III**](2nd%20Year/2nd%20Semester/Laboratórios%20de%20Informática%20III) Knowledge application about C and Java Language.
  <br>
  **Puzzle Video Game like Sokoban developed in C and Java Language.**
   
- :star: [**Object Oriented Programming**](2nd%20Year/2nd%20Semester/POO) Introduction the Object Oriented Paradigm, its formal basis and its main characteristics.
  <br>
  **Set of all solved Practical Exercises.**
  <br>
  **Simplified Version of the Uber App (UMER) developed in Java Language.**

- [**Operating Systems**](2nd%20Year/2nd%20Semester/Sistemas%20Operativos) Knowledge about Core Informatics.
  <br>
  **Set of all Slides and Practical Exercises.**

- :boom: [**Program Calculation**](2nd%20Year/2nd%20Semester/Cálculo%20de%20Programas) Learning competencies in the form of programming laws directly applicable to functional programming.
  <br>
  **Set of all Slides and Practical Exercises.**
  
- [**Leadership and Entrepreneurship**](2nd%20Year/2nd%20Semester/Lideranca%20e%20Empreendorismo) Knowledge about towards strengthening the innovative and proactive attitudes and to sensitize students to entrepreneurial initiatives as a means of creating collective wealth.
  <br>
  **Business Points of the FOCUS project.**


#### :three: **Third Year** 

- [**Numerical Methods and Nonlinear Optimization**](3rd%20Year/1st%20Semester/Métodos%20Numéricos%20e%20Otimização%20Não%20Linear) Numerical Methods and Nonlinear Optimization.
  <br>
  **Set of all Slides and solved Practical Exercises.**

- [**Computer Networks**](3rd%20Year/1st%20Semester/Redes%20de%20Computadores) Knowledge about basic concepts of computer networks, underlying technologies and protocols involved, providing an overview of their operation and use.
  <br>
  **Set of all Slides and solved Practical Exercises.**

- :star: [**Databases**](3rd%20Year/1st%20Semester/Base%20de%20Dados) Introduction of formal tools for a rigorous specification, verification and implementation of relational, deductive and object-oriented databases.
  <br>
  **Creation of a Database for a Cinema Ticket Office using MySQL Workbench.**

- [**Deterministic Models of Operational Research**](3rd%20Year/1st%20Semester/Modelos%20Determinísticos%20de%20Investigação%20Operacional) Knowledge about the methodology of Operations Research and the 'concept-philosophy' of modeling and optimization, through the study of the selected techniques.
  <br>
  **Set of all Slides.**
  
- [**Development of Software Systems**](3rd%20Year/1st%20Semester/Desenvolvimento%20de%20Sistemas%20de%20Software) Knowledge about Introduced Software Engineering Models, Processes and Methods.
  <br>
  **Set of all Slides and solved Practical Exercises.**
  <br>
  **App for configuration/order a total Customized Car.**
  
- [**Distributed Systems**](3rd%20Year/1st%20Semester/Sistemas%20Distribuídos) Knowledge advanced training in concurrent programming and an introduction to modeling, architectures and programming of distributed systems.
  <br>
  **Set of all Slides and solved Practical Exercises.**
  
  ---
  
- [**Computer Communications**](3rd%20Year/2nd%20Semester/Comunicações%20por%20Computador) Complements and completes the two previous curricular units in the area of computer communications area, on a bottom-up perspective, ie, starting from the lower layers of the reference model up to the highest layers, from the physical to the application layer.
  <br>
  **Set of all Slides and solved Practical Exercises.**

- [**Computer Graphics**](3rd%20Year/2nd%20Semester/Computação%20Gráfica) Introduction to 3D Computer graphics theory.
  <br>
  **Set of all Slides and Practical Exercises.**
  <br>
  **Development a Mini Scene Graph based 3D Engine.**
   
- [**Informatics Laboratories IV**](3rd%20Year/2nd%20Semester/Laboratórios%20de%20Informática%20IV) Knowledge application about C# Language.
  <br>
  **Development of the Home Pet app! which allows you to order canine/feline services at home.**

- :star: [**Languages Processing**](3rd%20Year/2nd%20Semester/Processamento%20de%20Linguagens) Introduction of the notion of formal language and concepts of regular expression (RE), automaton and their application to the development of Language Processors (LP's).
  <br>
  **Set of all Practical Exercises.**

- [**Stochastic Models of Operational Research**](3rd%20Year/2nd%20Semester/Modelos%20Estocásticos%20de%20Investigação%20Operacional) Introduction of the Operations Research perspective in the analysis of problems, by presenting a set of the most used techniques.
  <br>
  **Set of all Slides and Practical Exercises.**
  
- :star: [**Systems for Representation of Knowledge and Reasoning**](3rd%20Year/2nd%20Semester/Sistemas%20de%20Representação%20de%20Conhecimento%20e%20Raciocínio) Introduction of the paradigms of knowledge representation and logical reasoning, and its application in the design and implementation of intelligent systems and decision support.
  <br>
  **Set of all Slides and solved Practical Exercises.**

### 2nd Cycle Degree - Master's


#### :four: **Fourth Year** 

- **First Specialization Profile** Cryptography and Information Security

  - [**Cryptographic Technology**](4th%20Year/1st%20Semester/Perfil%201%20-%20Criptografia%20e%20Segurança%20da%20Informação/Tecnologia%20Criptográfica) Knowledge of the Symmetric and Asymmetric Cryptography using cryptographic libraries for encrypting secure systems.
    <br>
    **Set of all Practical Assignments.**
    
  - [**Security Technology**](4th%20Year/1st%20Semester/Perfil%201%20-%20Criptografia%20e%20Segurança%20da%20Informação/Tecnologia%20da%20Segurança) Introduction to security concepts and everything that can involve a Network, Application, Web and even an Operating System.
    <br>
    **Set of all Practical Assignments.**

- **Second Specialization Profile** Computer Graphics

  - [**Computer Vision**](4th%20Year/1st%20Semester/Perfil%202%20-%20Computa%C3%A7%C3%A3o%20Gr%C3%A1fica/Vis%C3%A3o%20por%20Computador) Development of Image Systems and Computer Vision and promote the analysis of practical case studies and simulation of real life situations.
      <br>
    **Set of all Practical Assignments.**
    
  - [**Lighting and Visualisation I**](4th%20Year/1st%20Semester/Perfil%202%20-%20Computação%20Gráfica/Visão%20e%20Iluminação%20I) Knowledge and application of topic relevant to real-time CG.
    <br>
    **Set of all Practical Assignments.**

- **Complementary Course Units**
  
  - :star: [**Web Application Development**](4th%20Year/1st%20Semester/UC%201%20-%20Desenvolvimento%20Aplicações%20WEB) Knowledge about the Node.js cross-platform, through the creation of websites. Use of Database for the construction of the Data API and subsequent connection with the Interface.
    <br>
    **Set of all Practical Exercises.**
    <br>
    **Development of simplified version of Web App Facebook (ISN) developed in JavaScript using Node.js technology.**

  - [**NoSQL Databases**](4th%20Year/1st%20Semester/UC%202%20-%20Bases%20de%20Dados%20NoSQL) In-depth knowledge of different types of Non-Relational Databases. Small study about the Oracle Database.
    <br>
    **Set of all solved Practical Exercises.**
    <br>
    **Analysis, planning and implementation of a Relational DBS(Oracle) and two Non-Relational (MongoDB and Neo4J), based on the famous SAKILA model.**

  - [**Lighting and Visualisation II**](4th%20Year/2nd%20Semester/Perfil%202%20-%20Computação%20Gráfica/Visão%20e%20Iluminação%20II) Knowledge and application of Ray Tracing, Path Tracing, Ambient Occlusion and Depth of Field techniques for rendering 3D images.

    **Set of all Practical Assignments.**

Creation and Test of Convolutional Neural Networks using the Data Augmentation and Transfer Learning technique. Study of Generative Models.
    <br>
    **Set of all Practical Assignments.**
